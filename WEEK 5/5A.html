Famous Temples in Andhra Pradesh

Tirupathi

A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara.

It is one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.

Located in the Tirumala hills, it attracts millions of devotees annually.

Srisailam

A historic temple located in the Srisailam forest.

It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas.

Known for its ancient architecture and spiritual significance.

Srikalahasti

A significant Hindu temple known for its architectural beauty.

It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the Pancha Bhoota Stalas.

Located in the town of Srikalahasti, it attracts devotees from all over the world.
